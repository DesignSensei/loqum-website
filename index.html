HOW LOQUM WORKS

Everything you need for seamless locum staffing

Verified Profiles

Every pharmacist on Loqum goes through profile verification before taking shifts. Pharmacies can review key details before confirming cover, while pharmacists build a trusted work profile over time.

Post and Manage Shifts

Pharmacies can create shift requests with the date, time, location, rate, and role details clearly stated. This keeps staffing needs organized instead of relying on scattered calls and messages.

Match With Pharmacists

Loqum connects pharmacies with available pharmacists, giving both sides clear, flexible shift options.

Booking Records and Invoices

Once a shift is confirmed, Loqum keeps the booking details in one place. After the shift, invoice records can be generated to reduce manual follow-up and keep both sides aligned.

What is Loqum?

Loqum is Nigeria's healthcare locum marketplace — a platform that connects verified pharmacists with pharmacies that need temporary cover. Pharmacists set their own rates and availability, while pharmacies get fast, reliable staffing on demand.

How does Loqum verify pharmacists?

Every pharmacist on Loqum goes through a professional identity verification process, including validation of their PCN (Pharmacists Council of Nigeria) registration. Only verified professionals can accept shifts.

How does invoicing work?

Loqum handles invoicing automatically. Once a shift is completed, an invoice is generated and sent — no back-and-forth, no paperwork. Pharmacists get paid and pharmacies get a clear record, all in one place.

Can I set my own rate as a pharmacist?

Yes. Loqum is built around rate transparency — pharmacists set their own hourly rate and pharmacies see it upfront before booking. No hidden fees, no awkward negotiations.

Is Loqum available outside Lagos?

Loqum is currently in early access and launching in Lagos first. We're expanding to other Nigerian cities soon — join the waitlist to be notified when we reach your area.
