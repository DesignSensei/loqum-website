CORE VALUES

What We Stand For

Every decision we make comes back to these principles.

Trust

Every pharmacist on Loqum is PCN-verified before they can accept a single shift. Trust isn't assumed — it's built in.

Transparency

Rates are set by professionals and visible upfront. No hidden fees, no awkward negotiations — just clarity on both sides.

Reliability

Pharmacies get cover when they need it. Pharmacists get paid without chasing anyone. The platform handles the hard part.

Collaboration

Loqum is built on the belief that the best outcomes come from open collaboration, shared knowledge, and one connected healthcare community.

Access

We're opening up a market that was previously run on personal contacts and phone calls — and making it available to everyone.

Integrity

We build honestly and hold ourselves accountable to the professionals and pharmacies who depend on us.

Our Mission

Replacing the Phone Call with a Platform

Our mission is to modernise healthcare staffing in Nigeria. We believe pharmacists deserve control over their careers — and pharmacies deserve reliable cover without the scramble. Loqum makes both possible, through professional verification, rate transparency, and automation that actually works in the Nigerian market.

Our Vision

A verified, on-demand healthcare workforce — across every city in Nigeria

We envision a future where every pharmacy in Nigeria can find a qualified locum in minutes — and every healthcare professional can build a flexible, fairly-paid career on their own terms. Loqum starts with pharmacists. It won't end there.
